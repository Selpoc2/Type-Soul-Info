How to rank up as a Soul Reaper



Trainee-Grade 3

Once you spawn in, you will be teleported to the Soul Society, you can grind here until Grade 5 and you will get the
"Senkaimon" which will teleport you to Karakura Town, though it is recommended to keep farming in the Soul
Society by interacting with the Quest Boards.

After you reach Grade 3, you will be required to join a Division to continue grading up. You can find them at the
Soul Society by pressing the , button (next to the M key) . Here is a list of the Divisions and what each of them do
(division quests that asks you to do thing at Karakura/Rukon will teleport you there, and then you'll need to find a
Division Duty board)

RECOMMENDATION: For an easier time grinding from Grade 3 up to Grade 2, go into Division 12(easiest one).


Grade 3-2

Once you join a Division, to rank up from Grade 3 to Semi-Grade 2, just continue with normal quests board. You
should also try out your Division Quest to get a feel of how hard it is. These can be found somewhere throughout
your Division and should be left of the normal quest board inside. If you feel like that quest is too hard, switch to a
lower Division(preferably 12).

Once you reach Semi-Grade 2, you cannot just continue to do normal quests and rank up. You will be required to
also grip a certain amount of members of the other factions (quincy or arrancar, visored do not count since they
are still shinigami), along with regular and division quests.

Grade 2

After reaching grade 2 you will be required to obtain your Shikai to continue grading up.
Once you obtain your Shikai, continue to do normal missions, division quests, and quincy/arrancar grips until Semi-Grade 1

(If you would like to check your progress, there is a hospital in K-Town where you can pay 1,000 Yen to check what other things you must do before ranking up)

Semi Grade 1-Elite Grade

After you completed your shikai fight, you continue to do board missions, division quests, and quincy/arrancar
grips until you reach elite grade. If you are struggling to get the grips required, it is recommended to farm a friend
of an opposing faction until you grade up. (If grips were the last part you need for a grade increase, you can
continue to do more to reach the required amount for the next grade increase, but you cannot do more beyond
that).

Bankai

Bankai will be obtained after defeated the bankai and successfully getting a screen that says "THE BLADE IS ME"

Your bankai fight will be very hard. Depending on what type of shikai you have, the greater rarity, the harder it is
If you fight your Bankai and lose at any point, you will be sent back to the real world and placed on cooldown for 1
hour. To rid this cooldown, kill any other player from opposing races(Arrancar/Quincy)
The best way to beat your bankai is to use your critical move (R on computer, L2 on console, Crit button on mobile ),
using a damaging move, then flashstep backwards, repeat. Depending on your element you will need to account
for additional attacks, such as Ice's AoE freeze move, which you can just flashstep away from, or Blood's ranged
move, which can be parried. This is one of the best cheese methods, as there are others but they are usually
patched within a couple days of discovery.

If you don't care, and are struggling with your bankai fight, you can do two things. The first is roll your weapon to
get twinblade, the twinblade bankai's crit is a counter move, and the AI automatically tries to blockbreak using a
heavy attack. When in a sticky situation hold block, it will try to blockbreak using its heavy, but will enter the
counter stance instead. Once it does this flash step back. Another thing you can do is respec fully into speed
(obviously don't use skill boxes unless you plan on staying as a speed build). Speed has a lot of moves that after
you blockbreak, will allow you to get a quick stun move off and give you time to flash step away. The best strategy
to use when doing either of these, is to flash step back, it will follow and the moment it gets close to you use your
heavy. It will try to block and get blockbroken, you can then use a speed move (if you spec into speed) and then
flash step away. It will follow soon after, and rinse and repeat until you obtain your bankai.

(While this is a better method, it doesn't make it easy by any standards, so good luck)
