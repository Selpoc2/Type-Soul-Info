Stage 1

To complete Stage 1 you have to complete one of the following

7 Clan War wins

13 Raid Wins

20 ranked wins

( there may also be a 4th way with like 20 hours spent in ranked im not sure)

Your progress in this step can be tracked with an npc in karakura town. This npc has multiple text boxes depending on your completion

"You will never become anything compared to what I am." - 0-19% completion

"A couple of kills won't get you anywhere. Stop bothering me." - 20-39% completion

"Why do you keep coming back? You're still pathetic." - 40-59% completion

"Maybe you're not so bad after all. But I'm still not impressed." - 60-79% completion

"You're quite unbelievable; do you know that?" - 80-99% completion

"Unequaled. Unrivaled. Go make me proud, lil bro." - 100% completion

Stage 2

To complete Stage 2, you must complete the following without dying.

Grip 15 Elite-Grade players of an opposing faction (Quincy or Arrancar)

Kill 1 World Boss (Jidanbo, Bawabawa, or [that one angel from evangelion])

Kill 80 Hollows

Dying while in Stage 2 or 2.5 will reset your Stage 2 Progress.

You must go to Karakura town and talk to Kisuke. He will transport you to a minigame in a room with multiple swords.

To win you need to find the 3 swords that are the real ones, the rest are fake.

You only have a couple chances to choose a fake sword before the game is over.

If you do choose a fake sword it will create a fleeting blue trail that will show you the direction of one of the real ones.

If you fail the minigame you have to wait 15 minutes, or to negate the wait time you can grip a Quincy or Arrancar
