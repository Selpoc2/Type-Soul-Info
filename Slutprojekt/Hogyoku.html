Hogyoku Fragment

A Hogyoku Fragment is an item that you are able to acquire from raids, ranked, ranked bosses or trading.



You have a chance to lose fragments upon death, it is reccomended to store them at Nelliel, who resides in Karakura Town.

Hogyoku Fragments are usable for the following:
Letz. (10 frags)
Visored. (10 frags)
Vastocar. (10 frags)
Gran Ray Cero. (9 frags)
Advance Shunko (9 frags and elite grade)
